The go-to places to buy vintage in NZ

RETRO ADDICTION

As its name suggests, Retro Addiction will give you your fix of all things art deco and vintage. The shop stocks all your retro essentials – from stylish clothing and fashion accessories to all kinds of wholesome homeware, furniture and décor. There is something for everyone in here whether you’re after men’s clothing, women’s clothing, pottery from Germany, art or glassware. Browse to your heart’s content and fill your house with interesting things.
ADDRESS: 849 New North Road, Auckland, New Zealand.
Website: Click me

ZIGGURAT

If you’ve got a taste for classic and contemporary designer labels, Ziggurat is the place to visit. This effortlessly-cool secondhand outlet has been in the business of selling vintage pieces since the 1980s. Their clothes are pricier than at other shops, but the quality and timelessness of each garment makes the investment worth it. Whilst many secondhand shops rely on the cheapness of the wares to lure in customers, it’s Ziggurat’s quality that draws in shoppers.
ADDRESS: 144 Cuba Street, Wellington, New Zealand.
Website: Click me

FERRY ANTIQUE CENTRE

The Ferry Antique Centre is as much an antique collector’s playground as it is a fashionista’s paradise. Among its treasured pieces you’ll find art-deco style dresses, fine porcelains, ties and other things. And if you can’t find what you’re looking for, the Ferry Antique traders will actually go out of their way to source it – the shop even has a little black book for keeping records of their customers’ requests.
ADDRESS: Ferry Antique Centre, 282 Ferry Rd, Waltham, Christchurch 8011, New Zealand.

SO VINTAGE

Here at So Vintage we are all about the history behind our eclectic range of genuine French and European antiques, vintage and retro furniture and home decoration – for us it’s L’original or nothing. We handpick our beautiful collection of authentic industrial pieces, French country furniture, retro lighting, homeware, collectables and curios containing a different assortment of items to inspire you.
ADDRESS: Clifton Rd, Te Awanga, Hastings, Hawke's Bay 4102.
Website: click me

WOT U WANT

This store has a bit of something for everyone here, with lots of kitchen appliances, furniture, art, clothing and collectables. A whole bunch of Kiwiana and other antiques, lots of stuff to get nostalgic over, too. I’ve found a few beautiful 60s coats that would have been shed from some hippies wafting through long ago.
ADDRESS: 66b Kenny St, Waihi.

THE ODDITY

Located in a dinky little town just out of Dunedin, a family run second-hand shop full of interesting things that make it feel like a museum more than a shop. Old kitchenware, furniture, tins, vinyl, retro signs, quirky toys etc. Be sure to go up the creaky stairs at the back up in to the attic for more treasures.
ADDRESS: 183 Main Road, Waikouaiti.
